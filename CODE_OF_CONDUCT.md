# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

[![Contributor Covenant](https://img.shields.io/badge/Contributor%20Covenant-2.1-4baaaa.svg)](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)

## Our Pledge
- We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone
- This pledge covers age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, and sexual identity and orientation

## Our Standards
- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward other people
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
- Accept responsibility and apologize to those affected by our mistakes
- Focus on what is best for the overall community
- Unacceptable behaviour includes sexualized language or imagery
- Unacceptable behaviour includes trolling, insults, and personal or political attacks
- Unacceptable behaviour includes public or private harassment
- Unacceptable behaviour includes publishing private information without permission
- Unacceptable behaviour includes other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities
- Project maintainers clarify and enforce standards of acceptable behaviour
- Maintainers take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to behaviour they deem inappropriate
- Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions
- Maintainers communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate

## Scope
- This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces
- It also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces

## Enforcement
- Report incidents to daniloaguiarbr@proton.me
- All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly
- The project team is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter

## Enforcement Guidelines
- Correction: private written warning with clarity about the violation
- Warning: interaction restrictions for a specified period
- Temporary ban: temporary ban from community interaction
- Permanent ban: permanent ban for pattern of violation or severe misconduct
